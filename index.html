A Judicial Luminary

Serving truth and justice with unwavering dedication

Judicial Philosophy

Justice Mohammed believes in a balanced approach to law, where the letter of the law meets the spirit of justice. Her rulings reflect deep understanding of both legal principles and human circumstances.

Court Leadership

Presiding in the historic Zaria Judicial Division, Justice Mohammed oversees cases at both the GRA Zaria and Dogarawa court locations, bringing wisdom and fairness to every proceeding.

Oath of Allegiance

Honored to administer the oath at NYSC ceremonies on behalf of the Chief Judge, symbolizing her role in guiding Nigeria's youth toward national service and integrity.

Legal Scholarship

With decades of experience, Justice Mohammed has contributed significantly to Nigerian jurisprudence, particularly in civil law, family law, and constitutional matters.

Journey of Justice

A distinguished path of service and wisdom

Early Career

Foundations of Law

Justice Mohammed began her legal journey with exceptional academic achievements, graduating with honors from law school and distinguishing herself in early legal practice.

Judicial Appointment

Ascending the Bench

Recognized for her exceptional legal acumen and integrity, Justice Mohammed was appointed to the Kaduna State High Court, beginning her distinguished judicial career.

Zaria Division

Serving the Historic City

Assigned to the Zaria Judicial Division, Justice Mohammed has presided over cases at the GRA Zaria and Dogarawa court locations, becoming a respected figure in the community.

NYSC Ceremonies

Guiding National Service

Honored with administering the oath of allegiance at NYSC ceremonies, Justice Mohammed plays a vital role in initiating Nigeria's youth into national service.

Present Day

Continuing Legacy

Today, Justice Mohammed continues to serve with distinction, balancing her judicial responsibilities with mentorship and community engagement.

To Our Matriarch of Justice

A Message from Your Loving Family

Dearest Mother and Wife,

Your life has been a masterclass in balancing strength with compassion, authority with humility, and wisdom with grace. As you preside over your courtroom, we see not just a judge, but a guardian of truth who approaches each case with the care of a mother and the wisdom of a sage.

Your hands that sign judicial orders are the same hands that comforted scraped knees, prepared family meals, and guided us through life's challenges. The same intellect that dissects complex legal arguments taught us our first lessons in integrity and fairness.

We've watched with pride as you've shaped the Kaduna judiciary, bringing not just legal expertise but profound humanity to the bench. Your commitment to justice has inspired each of us to pursue excellence in our own fields, always remembering the values you instilled in us.

To your grandchildren, you are "Grandma Justice" - a living symbol that fairness matters, that truth prevails, and that women can shape the world from any position. Your legacy is already taking root in the next generation as they learn from your example.

As you continue your distinguished service, know that your greatest courtroom isn't made of wood and law books, but of the lives you've touched - in our family, in your community, and across the judiciary. Your wisdom echoes through generations, your compassion transforms lives, and your integrity sets a standard we all strive to meet.

With Eternal Love and Admiration,
Your Family
